In this system we developed a “Smart Helmet” which detects the alcohol consumed by the rider and whether the rider has worn the helmet or not hopefully this system will provide the rider’s safety and restrict Drink n Drive condition and the traffic rules will also be followed.

VEHICLE TO VEHICLE COMMUNICATION

Vehicle to Vehicle Communication in this system, we are receiving the vehicle information such that breaking status, direction, speed and transmitting this to the vehicles in the vicinity vehicles. For breaking status Force sensing register is used. For direction accelerometer sensor is used and for speed calculation Hall Effect sensor is used. All this information is displayed on display in vehicle. Due to that driver or autonomous vehicle will get assistance and can take preemptive action so that he/she/it can drive safely.
